About

I'm an industrial engineering student who enjoys indulging in classic sci-fi and dystopian novels. From time to time I'll pick up a philosophy book or classic just for fun.

Some authors who's work has inspired me:

  • - Isaac Asimov
  • - Margaret Atwood
  • - Kurt Vonnegut
  • - Ray Bradbury
  • - Robert Heinlein

Unfortunately, I haven't written anything as of yet, but I do have a few ideas for potential dystopian novels. I'm interested in exploring the hard questions about our future - How will the economy run? What role technology play? How will our relationships with each other evolve? Hopefully, I can put together a disciplined effort to get something out to share with fellow Wattpaders!

Also, check out The Grand Signal (www.thegrandsignal.com). It's a news site that covers topics at the intersection of technology, human rights, and social change.
